about chirrp

built for students who want to speak clearly and show up ready.

chirrp is a practice space for communication skills: speaking prompts, interviews, debates, group discussions, and MUN-style preparation. The goal is simple: help students practice out loud in small, repeatable rounds.

small practice

Short rounds make it easier to begin, even when confidence is low or the topic feels unfamiliar.

real situations

Prompts are shaped around interviews, GDs, debates, and speeches students actually face.

visible growth

Streaks, XP, and skill areas make progress easier to notice and keep building.

why chirrp exists

Most students are told to be confident, but not always given a safe place to practice becoming confident. chirrp is designed as that place: playful, low-pressure, and useful enough to come back to every day.

come practice one round

Start with one prompt, then build from there. The first goal is not perfection. It is showing up and speaking.
